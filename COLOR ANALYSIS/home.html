How to Color Analyze Yourself

Step-by-Step Self Analysis

  • Step 1: Stand in natural daylight without makeup, wearing a white top.
  • Step 2: Hold different colored fabrics (warm and cool tones) near your face.
  • Step 3: Observe which colors make your skin glow, eyes sparkle, and reduce shadows.
  • Step 4: Note whether warm (gold, coral) or cool (silver, blue) tones flatter you more.
  • Step 5: Use these cues to find your seasonal palette: Spring, Summer, Autumn, or Winter.
